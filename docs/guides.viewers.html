Getting Started

Welcome to the Web3 Crypto Streaming Service! This guide will help you navigate our platform as a viewer, from setting up your account to discovering content and earning rewards.

What You'll Need

  • A crypto wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or other Ethereum-compatible wallet)
  • A small amount of cryptocurrency for transactions (ETH or our supported tokens)
  • A device with a modern web browser
New to Web3? Don't worry! Our platform offers a simplified "Viewer Mode" that allows you to browse and watch content without a crypto wallet. Some features will be limited, but you can upgrade anytime.

Creating Your Account

  1. Visit the platform website

    Navigate to web3streaming.io in your web browser.

  2. Click "Sign Up"

    Find the Sign Up button in the top-right corner of the homepage.

  3. Choose your account type

    Select "Connect Wallet" for the full Web3 experience, or "Email Signup" for Viewer Mode.

  4. Complete your profile

    Add a username, profile picture, and customize your viewing preferences.

Content Categories

Our platform organizes content into various categories to help you find what interests you:

  • Educational - Tutorials, courses, and informative content
  • Entertainment - Shows, performances, and creative works
  • Gaming - Gameplay, reviews, and esports
  • Music - Songs, concerts, and audio content
  • Podcasts - Audio discussions and interviews
  • Art & Design - Visual works and creative process
  • Technology - Tech reviews, coding, and digital innovation
  • Web3 Native - Blockchain and crypto-specific content
