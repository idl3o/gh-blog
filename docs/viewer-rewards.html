Staking Benefits

Staking your STREAM tokens provides additional benefits and accelerates your progress through viewer tiers:

Staking Rewards

  • Earn 8% APY on staked tokens
  • Receive bonus tokens when staking for longer periods
  • Automatic tier upgrades based on staking amount
  • Priority support and platform features

Staking Periods

  • Flexible: No lock-up, standard APY
  • 30 Days: 1.2x APY multiplier
  • 90 Days: 1.5x APY multiplier
  • 180 Days: 2x APY multiplier

How to Stake

To stake your STREAM tokens and start earning staking rewards:

  1. Navigate to your profile dashboard
  2. Select "Token Management" > "Staking"
  3. Choose the amount to stake and staking period
  4. Review the terms and confirm the staking transaction

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply create an account, connect your wallet, and start watching content. Rewards will automatically accumulate in your account based on your engagement activities.

Rewards are calculated in real-time but distributed to your account every 24 hours. You can view pending and processed rewards in your dashboard at any time.

There is a daily earning cap of 50 STREAM tokens per account to ensure fair distribution. This cap increases with your viewer tier.

Early unstaking from fixed-term staking periods incurs a 10% fee on earned rewards. Flexible staking has no early withdrawal penalty.
