Introduction

The Web3 Crypto Streaming Service is a decentralized platform built on blockchain technology that allows content creators and viewers to engage in a more direct, transparent, and equitable streaming ecosystem. Our platform leverages the power of cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and decentralized storage to create a new paradigm for content streaming.

Unlike traditional centralized streaming services, our platform eliminates middlemen, reduces fees, and gives content creators and consumers more control over their content and viewing experience.

Key Benefits
  • Lower Fees

    Direct creator-viewer relationships mean more revenue for creators and lower costs for viewers

  • Content Ownership

    Creators maintain full ownership of their content with immutable proof on the blockchain

  • Community Governance

    Token holders participate in platform decision-making through our DAO structure

  • Enhanced Privacy

    User-controlled data sharing and private viewing options

How It Works

Our platform connects content creators directly with viewers through blockchain-based smart contracts. All content is cryptographically signed, ensuring authenticity and ownership, while payments flow directly from viewers to creators with minimal platform fees.

Platform Architecture

The Web3 Crypto Streaming Service is built on these key technological pillars:

Smart Contracts

Our Ethereum-based smart contracts handle:

  • Content access controls
  • Payment processing and streaming payments
  • Proof of content existence and ownership
  • Governance voting and proposals
Learn more
Tokenized Economy

Our STREAM token powers the ecosystem:

  • Platform governance participation
  • Creator staking and rewards
  • Viewer engagement incentives
  • Network security through staking
Tokenomics
Decentralized Storage

Content is stored across a distributed network using:

  • IPFS for content addressing
  • Filecoin for persistent storage incentives
  • Redundant pinning services
  • Optional encrypted storage for private content
User-Friendly Interface

Our platform prioritizes accessibility:

  • Web and mobile applications
  • Simplified wallet integration
  • Familiar streaming interface
  • Progressive decentralization options
