Getting Started

Welcome to the Web3 Crypto Streaming Service! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know as a content creator on our platform. Before you begin, here's what you'll need:

  • Content ready to be uploaded (videos, audio, or other supported formats)
  • A crypto wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or other Ethereum-compatible wallet)
  • A small amount of ETH for transaction fees (or our supported L2 token)
Tip: If you're new to crypto wallets, we recommend starting with MetaMask due to its user-friendly interface and broad compatibility.

Account Setup

Setting up your creator account is straightforward and designed to give you full control over your content and earnings.

Creating Your Account

  1. Connect your wallet

    Navigate to the homepage and click "Connect Wallet" in the top-right corner. Select your wallet provider and follow the prompts to connect.

  2. Complete your profile

    Once connected, click on "Create Creator Profile" and fill out the required information:

    • Creator name (your brand or channel name)
    • Profile picture
    • Cover image
    • Bio/description
    • Social media links (optional but recommended)
  3. Choose your creator URL

    Select a unique URL for your creator page (e.g., web3streaming.io/creators/yourname)

  4. Set default monetization preferences

    Choose your default pricing model (more details in the Monetization section)

  5. Confirm your account

    Review your information and click "Create Account" to complete the process

Important: Your creator account is tied to your wallet address. Always ensure you have access to your wallet's recovery phrase and store it securely offline.

Creator Verification (Optional)

Verified creators receive additional benefits, including higher visibility, a verified badge, and access to exclusive features.

To become verified:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Verification
  2. Choose your verification method:
    • Proof of identity (KYC process)
    • Proof of social media following (linking accounts with significant following)
    • Proof of prior content creation (linking existing platforms)
  3. Follow the verification steps for your chosen method
  4. Wait for approval (typically 1-3 business days)

Content Upload

Our platform supports various content formats while ensuring your ownership is secured on the blockchain.

Supported Formats

Content Type Supported Formats Max File Size
Video MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM 10GB (standard), 30GB (verified creators)
Audio MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC 1GB
Images JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP 100MB per image, 2GB total album
Text/Documents PDF, EPUB, Markdown 500MB

Upload Process

  1. Prepare your content

    Ensure your content meets our community guidelines and is in a supported format

  2. Navigate to Creator Studio

    From your dashboard, click "Creator Studio" > "Upload Content"

  3. Upload your file

    Drag and drop your file or click "Browse" to select it from your device

  4. Add content details

    Fill out the title, description, tags, and thumbnail. Detailed metadata improves discoverability.

  5. Set monetization options

    Choose how viewers can access this specific content (free, one-time payment, subscription only, etc.)

  6. Review ownership registration

    Review the content hash and blockchain registration details that will secure your ownership

  7. Confirm upload

    Click "Publish" to initiate the upload and blockchain registration process

Tip: Uploading creates two processes: First, your content is encrypted and distributed to our decentralized storage network. Second, a content verification record is created on the blockchain. The second process requires a transaction fee (gas cost), which varies based on network conditions.

Monetization Options

Our platform offers multiple ways to monetize your content, giving you flexibility to choose what works best for you and your audience.

Monetization Models

Model Description Best For
Free (Ad-supported) Content is free to view, with non-intrusive ads shown to viewers. You earn a share of ad revenue. Building an audience, maximizing views
One-time Payment Viewers pay once for permanent access to specific content. Premium content, courses, special events
Subscription Viewers pay a recurring fee for access to all your content. Regular content creators with loyal audiences
Pay-per-minute Viewers are charged based on actual watch time. Longer-form content, educational material
Tips & Donations Viewers can send tips or donations while watching. Supplemental income for any content type
NFT Access Content access tied to ownership of your NFTs. Exclusive content, collectible experiences

Setting Up Monetization

  1. Configure payment wallet

    Go to Settings > Payments to confirm the wallet where you'll receive earnings

  2. Choose default monetization model

    Set your preferred model that will apply to all new uploads

  3. Set pricing

    Define your prices for each model you enable (subscription rate, one-time payment amounts, etc.)

  4. Configure individual content settings

    Override default settings on individual content if desired

Tip: Our platform fee is just 5-15% depending on your creator tier, compared to 30-50% on traditional platforms. The platform fee also includes gas cost optimization, so you don't have to worry about fluctuating network fees.

Setting Up Local Development Environment

While our main application is hosted at app.web3streaming.io, you can set up a local testing environment to experiment with the platform features without affecting your production content.

Local Domain Setup (Option 3)

To test the app locally with the same domain as production:

  1. Edit your hosts file

    Open your system's hosts file:

    • Windows: Open Notepad as administrator and open C:\Windows\System32\drivers\etc\hosts
    • macOS/Linux: Open terminal and run sudo nano /etc/hosts
  2. Add local domain mapping

    Add the following line to the file:

    127.0.0.1    app.web3streaming.io
  3. Save the file

    On Windows: Save in Notepad
    On macOS/Linux: Press Ctrl+O, Enter, then Ctrl+X

  4. Start a local web server

    You have several options:

    • Python: Navigate to your app directory and run python -m http.server 80 (requires admin/root)
    • Node.js: Use Express or http-server (npx http-server -p 80)
    • XAMPP/WAMP: Configure your DocumentRoot to point to your app directory
  5. Access your local app

    Open http://app.web3streaming.io in your browser

Tip: For testing with HTTPS, you'll need to set up a self-signed certificate. Consider using tools like mkcert to create locally-trusted development certificates.
Important: Remember to remove or comment out the hosts file entry when you want to access the real production site again.
